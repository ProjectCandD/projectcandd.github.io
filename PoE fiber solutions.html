Summary

Caterpillar (Building constructions product division) are leaders in manufacturing products like Backhoe loaders, Skid steer loaders by increasing their manufacturing footprint to India. Worked with Concepts & Devices to design an implementation of CommScope’s Powered Fiber Cable System for Video Surveillance (CCTV) Project. 90+ high definition security cameras have been installed successfully across the plant on the course at distances up to 800 meters from the central power source.

Challenges to overcome

Prime design was to run an optical fiber cable and a separate power cable from the UPS source to each CCTV camera poles and to power up with media converters, which make the design, manpower and additional material extravagant

Project Objective

To overcome the above subject we worked with Commscope Powered fiber cable system to facilitate the client on the requirement. So using this Commscope’s powered fiber cable system we can deliver reliable fiber optic signals to and from devices along with low voltage DC which simultaneously powers them

Design Consideration

IP based CCTV systems offer significant advantages. In order to take full advantage of the benefits of an IP CCTV system, the design and implementation of the solution needs to be carefully planned and executed. For IP systems, the video data travels across a network based infrastructure.

The distance ranges 150 meters to 800 meters between the cameras and switch; to avoid the insecure connectivity with the traditional mean of media converter here we go with this Commscope Powered Fiber Cable solution which can deliver DATA and POWER in the same medium with greater reliability with the PoE power extender to ensure the interoperability between the cameras and the switches.

So we selected 3 different distribution areas to establish the connectivity for all the CCTV cameras namely SSL, PDI & Security gate with the cable run count of 14,14 & 7 total of 35 CCTV locations respectively.

An Introduction to Powered Fiber Cable System

The Powered Fiber Cable System combines hybrid optical fiber and copper cabling plus electronics to provide a complete indoor/ outdoor solution for both powering and communicating with HD Cameras, Wi-Fi AP’s, small cells, and other PoE devices. It allows extending PoE from the standard 100 meters up to distances of 3000 meters or more. The system provides both remote electrical power and gigabit communications via hybrid optical fiber cabling, incorporates three levels of electrical protection, media conversion for converting from optical fiber to CAT cabling, and DC/DC electrical conversion to automatically correct for voltage drop over long distances

Deliveries & Execution

PFC, single mode, 4F, outdoor, 12AWG cable were delivered in the first phase and started with cabling routing for 35 CCTV locations as per the plan and all other materials in the pipeline delivered as per the schedule for the smooth execution within the given timeline.

Cables were laid with virgin HDPE pipe and clamped on the wall rather on the trench to keep the visibility of the cables. The clamps holding the HDPE pipes are coated with rust proof paints. Cables were connected as per the signed off plan with the IT team.
